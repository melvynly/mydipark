MYDI PARK RULES

COMPONENT

  • a board
  • a dice
  • tokens for each player
  • 25 yellow cards = easy
  • 25 blue cards = difficult

SETUP

Place the board on a table Place the two different types of questions on their allotted space on the board. Each player chooses one token to represent them while traveling around the board

OBJECT OF THE GAME

Each player throws the dice. The player with the highest total starts the play. Then each player places his token on the corner marked "GO", and throws the dice and moves the number of spaces indicated by the dice. You answer a question, there are two difficulty levels. Everytime you have the right answer, you gain a person and keep the card. If you donâ€™t have the right answer, you put the card back in the game. The tokens remain on the spaces occupied and proceed from that point on the player's next turn. Two or more tokens may rest on the same space at the same time. Be careful, some squares on the board make you lose a card, win a card, be arrested by the police...

GAMEPLAY

The "police" square: If you come across this square, you pass your turn once. The "-1" square: If you stop on this square, you lose one of your passengers. Put the card back in the game. If you don't have any yet, nothing happens. The "+1" square: if you stop on this square, you gain a passenger. If you already have 4 and your car is full: nothing happens. The "shortcut" square: If you stop exactly on this square, you can enter the parking lot and bypass the no entry sign
