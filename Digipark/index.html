Our History

Once upon a time, there was the Eduservices group.

They had several campuses throughout mainland France. The My Digital School in Vannes was highly reputed. An incredible, prestigious school, with the best students France had ever seen. Only problem. It was impossible to get there. The school parking lot can't accommodate all the students. So every morning it's a war The war for spaces! One day, seeing the misunderstanding between the students, Justine, in charge of pedagogy, came up with an idea to avoid any conflict. "What if we put some rules in place to stop this war?!" So she set about drawing up the new parking rules.

MY DIGITAL SCHOOL RULES
Rule 1: The parking lot is guarded by a security guard, Olivier.
Rule n°2: To access the parking lot guarded by Olivier, 5 people must be present in the car.
Rule 3: To win people along the way, questions are asked. A correct answer = one more person in the car.
Rule 4: Be careful: along the way, it's possible to win a person for free, lose a person, or be stopped by the police.
Rule 5: May the best person win!

Rules

MYDI PARK RULES

COMPONENT

  • - a board
  • - a dice
  • - tokens for each player
  • - 25 yellow cards = easy
  • - 25 blue cards = difficult

SETUP

Place the board on a table Place the two different types of questions on their allotted space on the board. Each player chooses one token to represent them while traveling around the board

OBJECT OF THE GAME

Each player throws the dice. The player with the highest total starts the play. Then each player places his token on the corner marked "GO", and throws the dice and moves the number of spaces indicated by the dice. You answer a question, there are two difficulty levels. Everytime you have the right answer, you gain a person and keep the card. If you don’t have the right answer, you put the card back in the game. The tokens remain on the spaces occupied and proceed from that point on the player's next turn. Two or more tokens may rest on the same space at the same time. Be careful, some squares on the board make you lose a card, win a card, be arrested by the police...

GAMEPLAY

The "police" square: If you come across this square, you pass your turn once. The "-1" square: If you stop on this square, you lose one of your passengers. Put the card back in the game. If you don't have any yet, nothing happens. The "+1" square: if you stop on this square, you gain a passenger. If you already have 4 and your car is full: nothing happens. The "shortcut" square: If you stop exactly on this square, you can enter the parking lot and bypass the no entry sign

SPECIAL RULES

Be the first one to park next to My digital School batiment to win the game.
